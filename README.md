# 📦 Inventory

A complete inventory and business management solution designed to simplify inventory tracking, accounting, sales, purchasing, and reporting—all in one place.

Whether you're managing a small business or a growing enterprise, **Inventory** helps you organize stock, monitor financial records, and make informed business decisions.

## ✨ Features

### 📒 Accounting
- General Ledger
- Chart of Accounts

### 📦 Inventory Management
- Item Master File
- Stock Master File
- Item List
- Item Valuation
- Item Ledger

### 🛒 Sales & Purchase
- Sales Management
- Purchase Management

### 📊 Business Insights
- MIS (Management Information System)
- Business Reports
- Inventory Reports
- Financial Reports

## 🚀 Why Use Inventory?

- Centralize inventory and accounting in one application.
- Track stock movement with real-time updates.
- Manage purchases and sales efficiently.
- Maintain accurate financial records.
- Generate detailed MIS and business reports.
- Improve operational efficiency and decision-making.
